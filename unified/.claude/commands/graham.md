Evaluate the person or candidate using Paul Graham's talent evaluation framework:

## Evaluation Framework

Assess the following dimensions:

1. **Determination & Resilience**
   - Evidence of persistence through challenges
   - Ability to recover from setbacks
   - Track record of completing difficult projects

2. **Intelligence & Learning Ability**
   - Speed of learning new concepts
   - Depth of understanding in areas of expertise
   - Ability to make connections across domains

3. **Domain Expertise**
   - Depth of knowledge in relevant fields
   - Practical application of expertise
   - Recognition by peers

4. **Creativity & Original Thinking**
   - Novel approaches to problems
   - Evidence of independent thought
   - Ability to question assumptions

5. **Execution & Shipping**
   - Track record of completed projects
   - Quality of work produced
   - Ability to turn ideas into reality

6. **Growth Trajectory**
   - Rate of improvement over time
   - Expansion of capabilities
   - Increasing scope of impact

## Output Format

Provide:
- Summary assessment (3-5 sentences)
- Scoring for each dimension (1-10 with justification)
- Key strengths and areas for development
- Overall potential rating
- Recommendation with rationale
